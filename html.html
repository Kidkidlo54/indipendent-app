Thailand

If you could go anywhere in the world where would it be? I would rather go to Thailand and I am going to explain useing P.E.P.S

Physical = Thailand is located in the heart of Southeast Asia. It has a total land size of 513,120km squared and is is under China and beside India. It has a climate of tropical wet and dry(or savanna kind of climate). Natural resourses coal, natural gas, gold, fluorite, lead, manganese, rubber, limestone, basalt, niobium, zinc, tin, tungsten, and gypsum.

Econimic = The total GDP per capita(average amount of money per person) is 7,189.04 USD. Along with that, the currency(type of money) is Thai Baht. The top imports of Thailand are Crude Petroleum ($18.3B), Integrated Circuits ($8.5B), Petroleum Gas ($7.09B), Vehicle Parts ($6.15B), and Gold ($5.12B), and top exports are Refined petroleum – $7.84 billion, Rice – $5.77 billion, Rubber – $4.63 billion, Gold – $4.41 billion, Diamonds – $1.52 billion.

Social= The Population size is 69.8 million in 2020 and the life expecency in Thailand is 77.15 years(data from 2019 due to covid). The Hmong, the Karen, the Lisu, the Mien, the Akha, the Lahu, the Lua, the Thin and the Khamu are the recognized indigenous peoples of Thailand. Major language is Thai. Pad Thai is probably the most famous Thai food dish. religions in Thailand are 85 to 95 percent of the population is Theravada Buddhist and 5 to 10 percent Muslim. The culture in Thailand is a mix of strong Indian influences, Chinese traditions, and elements that make The uniqe culture, Thai.
